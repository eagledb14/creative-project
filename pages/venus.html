Venus


The hottest planet

Venus Facts

Size

Venus is called Earth's twin because it's size and structure is the closest to Earth's.

Orbit

Venuses orbit is the second closest to the Sun. It revolves the Sun once every 225 Earth days.

Rotation

Venus rotates very slowly, each day being 243 Earth days, about 18 days longer than its year.

Atmosphere

Venus has a thick atmosphere that was caused by a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet in the solar system.

Friends

Every spacecraft that we have sent to the surface of Venus have melted in a matter of hours because of the intense heat from the atmosphere.
