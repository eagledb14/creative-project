Mars Overview

The Past

Through our many explorations of Mars, we have found a lot of evidence that Mars used to be much warmer and wetter than it is now.

Orbit

One revolution takes 687 Earth Days, but each martian day is about the same as Earths.

Atmosphere

Its atmosphere is mostly nitrogen and carbon dioxide, with very little oxygen present.

Life

Currently nothing can live there, but we are researching to see if it is possible in the future.

Friends

Mars is super busy. It has 2 natural moons and has been visisted the most by our space explorations in our solar system.
