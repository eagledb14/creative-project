Mercury


The closest planet to the sun

Mercury Overview

Size

Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system and is 38% the size of Earth.

Orbit

Mercury's orbit is the closest to the Sun. It revolves the Sun once every 88 Earth days.

Atmosphere

Mercury's atmosphere is very thin and is not suitable for humans to breathe.

Life

Because of Mercury's small atmosphere and extreme radiation from the Sun, it is unlikely that any life and be there.

Friends

Mercury has no rings nor moons, making it alone.
